Established by Messrs. Ronald Olivar Solis and Jose Gerardo A. Medina in 1994, our
Firm has earned a reputation for distinguished service by offering practical solutions
and inventive approaches to the concerns of a diverse clientele.

Our lawyers are graduates from the top law schools of the country with varied
background, talents, outlook, and persuasions, adding to our Firm’s dynamism.

We believe in a creative and solutions-oriented approach, accompanied by practical
analysis and good business sense in addressing clients’ concerns. Our culture fosters
out-of-the-box thinking in getting things done.

Our primary goal is to provide the highest quality of efficient and prompt legal services
to a wide variety of clients in an atmosphere of mutual trust, hard work, and
professionalism.

Our institutional clients include United Coconut Planters Bank (UCPB), UCPB General
Insurance Co., Inc., Petron Corporation, San Miguel Corporation, Inc., Hyder Consulting
Middle East Ltd., Pöyry Energy, Inc., Makati (Sports) Club, Inc., and Club Filipino, Inc. to
name a few.
