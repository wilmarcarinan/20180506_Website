We are steeped in civil, commercial, criminal, and specialized litigations before judicial and quasi-judicial bodies ranging from court
trial to appellate proceedings. We represent parties, among others, in lawsuits and proceedings involving civil and criminal matter,
intra-corporate controversies, contract breach and enforcement, election controversies, family relations, probate of wills, settlement
of estate, insurance claims, securities regulation, and, administrative and regulatory issues.

We provide a full range of legal services for clients in the field of business and commercial undertakings, including business
formation and maintenance, negotiations, venture capital and securities transactions, and services with respect to intellectual
property, mergers and acquisitions, bankruptcy creditor rights, real estate development, lease and sale transactions, and, taxation.
We provide for our clients prompt and practical advice on a cost-effective basis so as to facilitate their business concerns and
resolve issues at the earliest possible time.​

We have extensive experience in guiding clients through the government's legal and regulatory framework including issues and
concerns relating to foreign investments and government projects. The firm's experience in the various regulatory agencies of
government is reflected in the scope and diversity of our client base. We are well versed in the regulatory framework and
administrative procedures for natural resources utilization, oil, power and energy, transportation, aviation, real estate
development, intellectual property, and telecommunications. One of our founding partners was at the Chairman of the National
Telecommunications Commission from 2004 to 2007 when it tackled issues and regulations related to VOIP, 3G, Digital
Television, Broadband and WiMax utilization, VAS registration, and Competition Policy.

We provide comprehensive advice and assistance in projects and requiring a multi-disciplinary approach.Through our network and
linkages to local and international consultants and specialists, we are able to provide solutions in areas requiring technical expertise
and highly specialized training, knowledge, and experience. Among the projects handled by us in partnership with these experts are
those involving telecommunications and broadcast infrastructure, railways and airports development, renewable energy and power
generation, and, mining and exploration.​

We provide comprehensive advice and legal services in the field of labor and employment, particularly, labor standards and labor
relations, dispute resolution, and collective bargaining.We also assist individuals and foreign employees of corporations with respect
to their working visa, working permit, and other immigration issues​
